Blossom Word

A floral sky Wordle-style game with one five-letter word, six guesses, daily progress saving, and a random replay mode.

Type on your keyboard or tap the buttons below.

How to Play

Guess the hidden five-letter word in six tries.

After each guess, the tiles flip to show how close you are.

Green means the letter is in the right place. Gold means the letter is in the word but in a different spot. Gray means the letter is not in the word.
