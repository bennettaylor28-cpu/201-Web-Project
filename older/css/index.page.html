My Swimming Journey

I have been swimming since I was 9 years old. I trained for eight years with Hilltop Aquatics and spent my senior year of high school swimming with Wasatch Front Fish Market.

Swimming has brought so many meaningful people into my life, and I am very grateful for that. Through years of training and competition, I have developed discipline, resilience, and a deep appreciation for teamwork.

My primary events are the 50, 100, and 200 freestyle, where I focus on speed, technique, and consistency.

Competitive Highlights

I have competed at a high level in swimming, including:

  1. Winter Junior Nationals – 2024
    • Competed against top national-level swimmers
    • Specialized in sprint freestyle events
  2. Austin Sectionals – 2025
    • Competed in the 50, 100, and 200 freestyle events
    • Continued development in sprint performance
  3. BYU Swimming – Freshman Year
    • Team record holder in the 200 Freestyle Relay
    • Top 8 all-time at BYU in the 50 freestyle with a time of 23.01
    • BYU Richards Building Pool record holder in the 200 Freestyle Relay
